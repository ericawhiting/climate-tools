Future climate, 30 seconds spatial resolution¶

See the main page for background information and for other spatial resolutions. Downscaled monthly future climate data from CMIP6 is available in GeoTiff files for nine GCMs and four SSPs for the following time periods: 2021-2040, 2041-2060, 2061-2080, and 2081-2100. The climate variables available are tn - monthly average minimum temperature (°C), tx - monthly average maximum temperature (°C), pr - monthly total precipitation (mm), and bc - bioclimatic variables.
